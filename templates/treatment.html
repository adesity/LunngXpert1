PNEUMONIA

Symptoms :

  1. Cough, often producing phlegm
  2. High fever
  3. Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  4. Chest pain when breathing or coughing
  5. Fatigue and weakness
  6. Nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea (more common in bacterial pneumonia)
  7. Bluish lips or nails (a sign of oxygen deprivation, more severe cases)

Available Treatment :

Mayo Clinic
Verywell Health

COVID - 19

Symptoms :

  1. Fever or chills
  2. Dry cough
  3. Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  4. Fatigue
  5. Muscle or body aches
  6. Headache
  7. Loss of taste or smell
  8. Sore throat
  9. Congestion or runny nose
  10. Nausea or vomiting, and diarrhea (less common but reported)
